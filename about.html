About Ikojn Fashion

Our Story

Ikojn Fashion was started in 2010 by Ivy Maithya in Nairobi City. We make Unique and Comfortable Clothes for men, women and children.

Welcome Ikojn Fashion to where fashion meets personality. We're a modern clothing brand dedicated to providing stylish, high-quality, and affordable wear for men, women, and children. Whether you’re dressing for everyday comfort or a special occasion, we have something designed just for you..

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals through fashion by providing stylish, affordable, and inclusive clothing for men, women, and children. We aim to inspire confidence and self-expression in every outfit, while maintaining a commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction..

Our Vision

To become a leading fashion brand known for empowering style, celebrating diversity, and making fashion accessible to all. We envision a world where everyone feels confident, included, and inspired—one outfit at a time.

What We Believe In

Style with Purpose

We believe fashion should reflect individuality, culture, and confidence.

Inclusivity

We design for all ages, sizes, and identities—everyone deserves to feel seen and stylish.

Quality Craftsmanship

Every stitch and seam reflects our commitment to durability and excellence.

Affordability

We keep our prices fair because great fashion should be within everyone’s reach.
